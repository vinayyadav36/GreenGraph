# PRODUCT OVERVIEW

SALTEDHASH is a self-contained educational platform for foundational learning, exam preparation (NEET/JEE), and entry-level career readiness.

## 1) Fundamentals Lab
Learners browse concepts by subject and grade band, open a concept, and learn through stepwise structured explanations. Practice questions are tied to concepts and solutions are intentionally gated until learners attempt first.

## 2) Exam Engine
Learners select exam and topic, attempt one question at a time, and receive immediate correctness feedback plus stepwise solutions. Attempts are persisted locally for progress visibility.

## 3) Career Launchpad
Learners pick a resume template, fill structured sections, and save resume JSON to the local profile store. A selected job description highlights matched keywords for practical tailoring.

## 4) Project Studio (Stub)
A minimal list of projects for future expansion.

## Operating Model
- Offline-only workflow.
- JSON files in `/data` as NoSQL-like source of truth.
- No SQL, no external DB services, no third-party APIs.
