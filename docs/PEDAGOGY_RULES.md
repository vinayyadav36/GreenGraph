# PEDAGOGY RULES

## Teaching Principles
1. Teach process before answer.
2. Encourage learner attempt before solution reveal.
3. Present explanations stepwise, not as single opaque outputs.
4. Maintain conceptual clarity through short, structured instructional steps.
5. Keep content aligned with foundational understanding and exam readiness.

## Applied Product Rules
- Fundamentals and exam flows require an attempt or explicit learner action before full solutions are shown.
- Explanations are stored and shown in ordered steps.
- Interface language and interactions prioritize learning support over answer dumping.
- No one-click homework-solving features.
